How does it work?

1) To initiate a transaction, input the desired amount and select the specific coin you wish to send in the designated "You send" field. 
2) Determine the coin you want to receive and enter the corresponding receiving address. 
3) Opt for your preferred exchange rate type, either floating or fixed. Additional information on exchange types can be found below. 
4) Simply click the "Exchange" button to proceed. 
5) Transfer the trade amount to the indicated Deposit address. 
6) Your exchange will be automatically processed, and the funds will be deposited into your receiving address.

How we ensure the security of your funds and personal data?

Kriptum is a service that respects your privacy and values your security. As a non-custodial and registration-free platform, we don't ask for any personal or financial information. Your crypto assets are kept safe in your own wallet, and trades are conducted automatically, without any need for manual intervention. To ensure the highest level of security, we have implemented several measures, including an SSL certificate to protect against data interception, strong security protocols for all procedures, and protection against DDoS attacks. Rest assured that your assets and information are in safe hands with Kriptum.
